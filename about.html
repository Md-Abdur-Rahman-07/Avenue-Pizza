About Avenue Pizza

Our History

Founded in 1995, Avenue Pizza started as a small family-owned pizzeria with a passion for crafting authentic, handcrafted pizzas. Over the years, we've grown into a local favorite, known for our commitment to fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes.

What began as a humble pizza shop with a single oven and a handful of cherished family recipes quickly became a go-to spot for pizza lovers. Word spread about our crispy, hand-tossed crust, signature house-made sauces, and generous toppings, and soon, we had a loyal community of customers coming back for more.
